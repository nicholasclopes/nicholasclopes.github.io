A3 Refrigerant Leakage Analysis

CFD-based study of refrigerant leakage scenarios in confined residential spaces. The work focused on dispersion dynamics, ventilation effects, and safety thresholds.

  • 3D transient CFD simulations of refrigerant release
  • Risk mapping under different ventilation conditions
  • DOE-funded energy efficiency & safety project

Tools: ANSYS Fluent, Python, HPC clusters
